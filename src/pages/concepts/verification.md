Verification is the habit of matching claims to evidence. In blueForge, receipts and verification blocks are the product expression of that habit.

A **receipt** is a structured record of a tool or model call: inputs, outputs, and timing. A **verification block** (see reference) is the full compare surface when multiple models answer the same prompt.

This page stays short on purpose. Use the [verification block reference](/reference/verification-block) when you need field-level detail.
