Receipt verification means checking that the recorded tool inputs and outputs match what you see on disk.

Advanced workflows may compare cryptographic hashes across machines. Those flows ship behind feature flags: watch release notes for your edition.

Until then, treat the receipt log as the source of truth for what Apprentice attempted.
