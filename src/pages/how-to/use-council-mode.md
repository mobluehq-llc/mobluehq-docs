Council mode runs more than one model against the same prompt and surfaces differences before you merge.

## Enable

Open Settings, find **Council** (or multi-model review), and choose which providers participate. You need at least two configured providers.

## During a run

Smith shows each answer side by side. Pick the line of reasoning you want Apprentice to follow, or ask for another round.

## When to skip it

Small edits and known-safe refactors may not need council. Use judgment: council adds latency and cost.
